## Purpose of the Study

You are being invited to participate in a research study about how developers build and orchestrate AI agents. The purpose of this study is to understand the challenges, needs, and workflows of developers working with AI agent frameworks such as LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, and others.

This research will inform the development of AI Workflow Fabric, an open-source tool for orchestrating AI agents across frameworks.

---

## What You Will Be Asked to Do

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:

1. **Complete a screening survey** (5 minutes) to confirm eligibility
2. **Participate in a video interview** (60 minutes) discussing your experience with AI agents
3. **Optionally provide follow-up feedback** via email or short survey

---

## Recording

With your permission, the interview will be **audio and video recorded** for later analysis. The recording will be:

- Stored securely with restricted access
- Used only for research purposes
- Deleted after the study concludes (within 12 months)

You may request that recording be stopped at any time.

## Confidentiality

Your responses will be kept **confidential**. Specifically:

- Your name and identifying information will be replaced with a participant ID (e.g., P001)
- Any quotes used in reports or publications will be anonymized
- Your company name will not be disclosed without explicit permission
- Data will be stored securely and accessed only by the research team

## Voluntary Participation

Your participation is **completely voluntary**. You may:

- Decline to answer any question
- Take breaks at any time
- Withdraw from the study at any point without penalty
- Request that your data be deleted

---

## Risks and Benefits

**Risks**: There are no known risks beyond those of a typical conversation. Some questions may ask about work challenges, which could cause minor discomfort.

**Benefits**: 
- You will receive a **$50 Amazon gift card** as a thank-you for your time
- Your input will directly shape a tool designed to address developer pain points
- You may gain insights from reflecting on your own workflows
